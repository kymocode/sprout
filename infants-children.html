Early Nutrition: A foundation for growth and development

During the first few years of life, our little one’s brains and bodies undergo rapid development, and the right nutrients play a key role in supporting brain function, immune health, and physical growth.

Good nutrition in the early years sets the stage for long-term health, reducing the risk of chronic conditions later in life and promoting a healthy relationship with food as they grow. With postgraduate training in paediatric dietetics, Sprout Family Dietetics can support you and your little people with the following needs:

  • Faltering growth (slower than expected infant growth)
  • Growth and nutritional intake assessment
  • Infant tube feeding
  • Eating disorders
  • Diabetes
  • Fussy eating and improving food diversity
  • Constipation and gastrointestinal issues
  • Micronutrient Ddeficiencies
