Project 12 – One year: one you

Twelve months to transform your health, build habits that stick, and keep you moving toward the results you want with confidence

Let’s face it. There are endless online programs out there promising quick fixes and overnight results. But the truth is, real lasting change takes time. Building healthy habits that stick for life requires personalised support, accountability, and care.

When it comes to improving your health, I’m here for the long haul — no fad diets, no “all-or-nothing” plans, and no programs that stop working the moment you finish.

That’s why I created Project 12.

Project 12 provides a full year of private dietetic support, designed to make sustainable changes that last.

By commuting to 12 months of self-care you’ll receive:

  • 1 x 60-minute initial consultation to set your goals and create your roadmap
  • 11 x 30-minute monthly follow-up sessions to keep you on track
  • Personalised nutrient targets tailored to your needs
  • Weekly recipe drops to your inbox to keep you inspired in the kitchen. By the end of the year you will have over 50 recipies to keep you motivates and on track
  • 1:1 support that fits your lifestyle and helps you stay accountable

Valued at $1200, project 12 is discounted to $920.

To redeem this offer, a 50% deposit is required on your initial consultation, with the remaining cost split over your remaining 11 consultations ($41.80 per consultation)

This cost may be further reduced in one of two ways:

  1. If you are eligible for a GP Chronic Condition Management plan - you can receive up to $309 subsidy from Medicare split across 5 sessions. You will need to speak with your GP to determine if you are eligible
  2. If you have private health insurance you will receive a rebate from your fund- this varies depending on your level of cover.
